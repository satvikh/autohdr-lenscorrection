02
The Problem

Lens Distortion
Without Profiles

Barrel and pincushion distortion warp straight lines. Standard correction requires EXIF lens profiles — often unavailable.

  • Straight lines bow outward (barrel) or inward (pincushion)
  • Wide-angle lenses show the strongest radial distortion
  • No universal correction exists without the lens model
  • Challenge: correct any image with no prior knowledge
Distortion Types
Barrel
Radial expansion outward. Common in wide-angle lenses. k₁ < 0.
Pincushion
Radial compression inward. Common in telephoto lenses. k₁ > 0.
03
Why It Matters

Geometry is
a Trust Signal

  • Real estate photography
    Buyers expect straight walls. Barrel distortion reads as amateur.
  • Architectural imaging
    Structural lines must be preserved for technical accuracy.
  • User experience
    Distorted images feel cheap. Correct geometry signals quality.
  • Medical & AR/VR
    Precise geometry is a safety and accuracy requirement.
Key Insight

"A corrected image is not just more beautiful — it is geometrically honest. Straight lines are the fundamental promise of a trustworthy photograph."

07
Data Pipeline

Paired Dataset
& Stratified Splits

  • PairedLensDataset
    Reads CSV (image_id, input_path, target_path). Returns float32 RGB [C,H,W] in [0,1].
  • Dataset Audit
    Per-pair: image sizes, MAE proxy, edge density (Canny), Hough line richness
  • Stratified Splits
    Bins by resolution × line richness — ensures balanced train/val distribution
  • PairedTransforms
    Resize, crop, hflip — identical operations applied to input and target
