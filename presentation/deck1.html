Problem

Lens Distortion
Without Profiles

Barrel and pincushion distortion bow straight lines — but standard correction requires EXIF lens profiles that are often unavailable.

  • Straight lines appear curved near image edges
  • Wide-angle lenses exhibit strong radial distortion
  • No universal fix without knowing the exact lens model
  • Challenge: correct any image with no prior knowledge
BARREL DISTORTION
Radial expansion: lines bow outward. Common in wide-angle lenses.
OUR APPROACH
Predict warp parameters directly from the distorted image — no profile required.
Why It Matters

Geometry is
a Trust Signal

  • Real estate photography
    Buyers expect straight walls. Distortion reads as unprofessional.
  • Architectural imaging
    Structural lines must be preserved for technical accuracy.
  • User experience
    Distorted images feel low-quality. Correct geometry signals care.
  • Medical & AR/VR
    Precise geometry is a safety and accuracy requirement.
KEY INSIGHT

A corrected image is not just more beautiful — it is geometrically honest. Straight lines are the fundamental promise of a trustworthy photograph.

Strategy

Predict a Warp Grid,
Not Pixels

Instead of generating corrected pixels, we predict where each output pixel samples from the distorted input — a backward warp. No hallucination, pure geometry.

Data Pipeline

Paired Dataset
& Stratified Splits

  • PairedLensDataset
    Reads CSV (image_id, input_path, target_path). Float32 RGB [C,H,W] in [0,1].
  • Dataset Audit
    Per-pair: image sizes, MAE proxy, edge density (Canny), Hough line richness
  • Stratified Splits
    Bins by resolution × line richness — balanced train/val distribution
  • PairedTransforms
    Resize, crop, hflip — identical ops applied to both input and target

Training

Losses Aligned
to Geometry

  • Stage 1 — Parametric Head
    Edge loss + gradient orientation loss. Trains core Brown-Conrady correction.
  • Stage 2 — Residual Head
    Adds SSIM + smoothness regularizer. Local flow for fine detail.
  • Stage 3 — End-to-End
    Full pipeline fine-tuning with combined metric-aligned loss weights.
