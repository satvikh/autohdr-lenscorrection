problem_statement

Lens Distortion
Without Profiles

Barrel and pincushion distortion bow straight lines. Standard correction requires EXIF lens profiles — often unavailable in practice.

  • Lines curve near image edges due to radial distortion
  • Wide-angle lenses exhibit strongest barrel effect
  • No universal fix without exact lens model data
  • Goal: correct any image with zero prior knowledge
distortion_types
BARREL_DIST
Radial outward expansion. k1 < 0. Common in wide-angle.
PINCUSHION_DIST
Radial inward compression. k1 > 0. Common in telephoto.
